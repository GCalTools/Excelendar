- **Website**: [excelendar.com](https://excelendar.com)
- **MS Marketplace**: https://marketplace.microsoft.com/en-us/product/office/WA200009812

## Take control of your Outlook calendar with the power and speed of Excel.

Excelendar brings your Outlook calendar into Excel online or Desktop, giving you the power to view, edit, and manage your events in a familiar spreadsheet format. Bulk edit, analyze, and enrich your calendar data in minutes, not hours.


### Who is it for?
- Professionals analyzing time and meeting patterns
- Anyone who prefers calendar data in spreadsheet format
- Users who need to bulk-edit multiple events
- Users tracking custom data for calendar events
- Anyone creating events from existing spreadsheet data
